## PI

### arctan series

$$
    arctan'x = \frac{1}{1+x^2} \\
    {\frac{1}{1+x^2}}'= \frac{-2x}{(1+x^2)^2} \\
    {\frac{-2x}{(1+x^2)^2}}' = \frac{-2}{(1+x^2)^2} + \frac{8x^2}{(1+x^2)^3}  \\
    (\frac{-2}{(1+x^2)^2} + \frac{8x^2}{(1+x^2)^3})' = \frac{8x}{(1+x^2)^2} + \frac{16x}{(1+x^2)^3} + \frac{-48x}{(1+x^2)^4} \\
$$
